## 海报1：Hero 主视觉（暗调 Desk Setup 场景）

**用途**：首页 Banner / 社媒封面 / Amazon Brand Story 头图

**Prompt：**

```
A cinematic product photography shot of a premium T-shaped desk lamp in a high-end home office setup. The lamp has a sleek space gray matte aluminum finish with a long horizontal light bar (approximately 49cm) mounted on a vertical rectangular pole, connected at a clean right angle, standing on a square flat metal base. The light bar emits warm natural light downward, illuminating the desk surface with a soft glow.

The lamp stands tall (67cm height) behind and above a 34-inch curved ultrawide monitor on a walnut wood desk. A MacBook Pro sits to the left, its screen reflecting soft light. The room is dark with deep shadows, and the desk lamp is the primary light source, creating dramatic volumetric lighting. The wall behind has a subtle dark charcoal texture.

Style: dark moody commercial product photography, cinematic lighting, shallow depth of field, 16:9 aspect ratio, 4K resolution, shot on Phase One medium format camera. Color palette: space gray, warm white light, dark walnut wood tones.
```
